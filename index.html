TIC-TAC-TOE


Let's Play the Tic-Tac-Toe Game!


Players take turns putting their marks in empty square. The first player to get 3 of her marks
in a row (up,down,across,diagonally) is the winner. When all 9 squares are full, the game
is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game end in a tie.
